HGBCians' Hymn


(Intro)

In HGBaquiran College, we stand with pride,
Where knowledge and values coincide.
With skills and attitude, we stride the way,
To be the best in every single day.

(Chorus)

HGBaquiran College, our alma mater true,
We'll shine with knowledge, thanks to you.
Locally and globally, we'll compete,
For a productive life, we can't be beat.

Nation-building, our goal so clear,
With hearts devoted, we're always near.
We'll face the challenges, both near and far,
With creativity and strength, like a shining star.

(Repeat Chorus)


Social and moral tests, we'll overcome,
In this modern world, we'll be second to none.
With honor and integrity, we will prevail,
HGBaquiran College, you set the sail.

(Outro)

HGBaquiran College, in our hearts you'll stay,
Guiding our path, come what may.
With the mission and vision in our soul,
As alumni, we'll reach every goal.
