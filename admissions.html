Admissions


Welcome

We warmly welcome senior high school graduates, college transfers, second-year students, and international applicants to explore and join our vibrant campus. Submission of the following requirements to the Registrar's Office is mandatory for admission to any academic program:


We are dedicated to producing quality graduates and ensure that every student meets our academic standards. To join our community, applicants must meet the following basic requirements:

  • • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • • Form 137 / Form 138 (Original Copy)
  • • Certificate of Good Moral Character (Original Copy)
  • • Drug Test (get a referral letter from the Registrar's Office)
  • • Four (4) pieces of 2x2 photos with blue background and name tag

We look forward to supporting your journey toward success!


We are excited to welcome you as you continue your educational journey with us. In line with our commitment to producing well-rounded and high-quality graduates, we carefully evaluate all student applicants for admission. To ensure that you are part of this esteemed institution, applicants must meet the following basic requirements:

  • • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • • Honorable Dismissal (Original Copy)
  • • Certificate of Grades/TOR (Original Copy)
  • • Certificate of Good Moral Character (Original Copy)
  • • Drug Test (Get a referral letter from the Registrar's Office)
  • • Four (4) Pieces of 2x2 Picture with Blue Background and Name Tag
