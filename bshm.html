HOTEL MANAGEMENT

Who are BSHM?

HGBaquiran College offers a dynamic Bachelor of Science in Hotel Management (BSHM) program tailored to prepare students for diverse careers in the hospitality industry. Led by experienced faculty members with industry expertise, our program provides a comprehensive curriculum covering key areas such as hotel operations, food and beverage management, hospitality marketing, and event planning. Students in our BSHM program benefit from a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical training, including hands-on experiences in our state-of-the-art facilities and through internships with leading hospitality establishments. Through these opportunities, students develop the essential skills and competencies needed to excel in various roles within the dynamic and fast-paced hospitality sector.

Moreover, our BSHM program emphasizes the importance of customer service excellence, business acumen, and cultural competency. Students learn to anticipate and meet the needs of diverse guests, analyze industry trends, and adapt to changing market demands. Additionally, our program instills a strong foundation in ethical leadership and sustainability practices, preparing students to contribute positively to the growth and development of the global hospitality industry. Whether students aspire to pursue careers in hotel management, resort operations, event planning, or hospitality entrepreneurship, the BSHM program at HGBaquiran College provides a solid foundation for success.

Join us and embark on a rewarding journey towards a fulfilling career in the exciting world of hospitality management.
