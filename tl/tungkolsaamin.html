ABOUT US

History

Established in 2005, HGBaquiran College stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of Tumauini, Isabela. With accreditation from both the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), our institution has consistently delivered exceptional learning experiences to students across various fields of study.

Our undergraduate programs span a diverse range, including teacher preparation, business administration, criminology, information technology, and hospitality management. Additionally, we offer a Senior High School curriculum tailored to meet the demands of the modern workforce, featuring a Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track with specialized strands in Higher Education (HE) and Information and Communications Technology (ICT).

Dedication, diligence, determination, devotion, and discipline are the guiding principles that underpin every facet of our institution. With these values at the forefront of our educational philosophy, we strive to inspire and equip our students with the tools they need to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

At HGBaquiran College, our ethos revolves around the cultivation of disciplined minds and the nurturing of globally competitive individuals. Our mission is rooted in the belief that education is not just about imparting knowledge but also about instilling values, honing skills, and fostering resilience to empower students to make meaningful contributions to society.

About the Logo

The logo for HGBaquiran College features a central shield, predominantly displaying the institution's
key elements. At the top of the shield, the initials "HGB" indicate the name of the college.
Below these letters, a graduation cap signifies the academic nature and educational focus of the institution.

Central to the shield is a red maple leaf, symbolizing Canada, which likely points to the college's location
or its affiliation with Canadian values and education standards. Overlaid on the maple leaf is an anchor
and sword, representing strength, stability, and preparedness—qualities that the college aims to instill
in its students. The establishment year "2005" is inscribed within the shield, marking the year the college
was founded. Surrounding the shield is a green laurel wreath, traditionally associated with victory
and achievement, emphasizing the college’s commitment to fostering success in its students.
The overall color scheme includes green, red, and white, which together evoke a sense of tradition, excellence, and a welcoming environment.
