Types of Cookiesüç™

There are various types of cookies that perform different functions and generally enhance your experience on our website. We may use cookies to determine whether you have visited the site before or to remember your preferences while using the site. For instance, we might store your chosen language in a cookie, allowing us to automatically select this language when you return to the website.

The types of cookies used on this website can be classified into one of three categories:

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for our website to function properly, enabling you to browse and use certain features of the site. They are typically set in response to actions you take that constitute a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling out forms. Without these cookies, some services you request, such as making online admission, cannot be provided. These cookies do not collect information about you that could be used for marketing or tracking your internet activity.
2. Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make, such as your username, or language and provide enhanced features. They improve your experience on our site, enhance its functionality, and enable you to customize the site to your preferences.
3. Diagnostic and Usage Cookies: These cookies collect diagnostic and usage data, including information about how visitors use and interact with our website. For example, they track the number of visits to the site or the average duration of a visit. We use this data solely to improve the website's design, performance, and stability.

Most of our cookies are first-party cookies, which are set by our website. However, we also use some third-party cookies, which are set by external websites. In our lists of example cookies, we clearly identify which ones are third-party cookies.

Use of IP Addresses: An IP address is a numeric code that identifies your device on the Internet. We use your IP address and browser type to help analyze usage patterns, diagnose problems on this website, improve the services we offer, and ensure the security of the website.
