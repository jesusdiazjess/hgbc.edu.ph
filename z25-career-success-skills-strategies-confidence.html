Batch 2025 Graduates Gear Up for Careers with Pre-Employment Seminar

Published May 21, 2025


In a proactive move to prepare its graduating students for the professional world, HGBaquiran College successfully conducted its Pre-Employment Seminar, a vital event that marks the beginning of their transition from campus life to career paths.

Held under the inspiring theme "Equipping Knowledge for Career Success: Skills, Strategies, and Confidence," the seminar went beyond conventional talks. It was a comprehensive and engaging guide designed to equip students with essential tools, practical knowledge, and the mindset to thrive in today’s competitive job market.

“This event is not just a requirement—it’s an investment in our future,” said a representative from the IT Department in an ambush interview, emphasizing the importance of preparation and professionalism.

The Pre-Employment Seminar-Workshop is one of the college’s flagship initiatives to ensure that every graduate leaves not only with academic credentials but also with a clear sense of direction and readiness for their chosen careers.
