CRIMINOLOGY

Who are Criminology?

HGBaquiran College takes pride in offering a distinguished program in Criminology, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of the complex dynamics of crime, justice, and societal responses. Our Criminology program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical principles necessary to pursue careers in law enforcement, criminal justice administration, crime analysis, and related fields. Through a blend of theoretical coursework, practical training, and experiential learning opportunities, students are prepared to critically analyze crime and deviance, understand the root causes of criminal behavior, and contribute to the development of effective crime prevention and intervention strategies.

Our Criminology program, led by experienced faculty experts, offers a multidisciplinary approach integrating sociology, psychology, law, and more. Students delve into criminological theories, criminal law, forensic science, and victimology, gaining diverse insights into the justice system. Through hands-on experiences like internships and simulations, they develop essential skills for success in their careers. Our emphasis on ethical decision-making and community engagement fosters compassionate leaders ready to address issues of equity and social justice within the criminal justice system. Whether students aspire to pursue careers in law enforcement, corrections, crime analysis, victim advocacy, or other related professions, the Criminology program at HGBaquiran College provides a solid foundation for success.

Join us and become a catalyst for positive change in the criminal justice system and society at large.
