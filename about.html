ABOUT US

History

In Tumaini, Isabela, HGBaquiran College has been providing top-notch education since 2005. Recognized by both the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), the college offers undergraduate programs in teacher preparation, business administration, criminology, information technology, and hotel and restaurant management. Additionally, it provides a Senior High School curriculum with a Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track, including the HE and ICT strands.

At HGBaquiran College, we prioritize disciplined minds and shaping globally competitive individuals. Our mission is to provide quality education that fosters values, skills, and resilience for meaningful contributions to society.

About Us

HGBaquiran College, Inc., is located in Tumauini, Isabela, Philippines 3325.
It was established in 2005 and was the town of Tumauini's only college.
