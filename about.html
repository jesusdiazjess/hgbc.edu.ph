ABOUT US

History

Established in 2005, HGBaquiran College stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of Tumauini, Isabela. Accredited by both the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), the institution has consistently delivered exceptional learning experiences to students across various fields of study.

HGBaquiran College offers a diverse range of undergraduate programs, including teacher preparation, business administration, criminology, information technology, and hospitality management. In addition, the college provides a Senior High School curriculum designed to meet the demands of the modern workforce, featuring a Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track with specialized strands in Higher Education (HE) and Information and Communications Technology (ICT).

The institution is guided by the core values of dedication, diligence, determination, devotion, and discipline, which are central to its educational philosophy. With these principles, HGBaquiran College aims to inspire and equip its students with the skills needed to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

The college's ethos focuses on cultivating disciplined minds and nurturing globally competitive individuals. Its mission is rooted in the belief that education is not merely about imparting knowledge but also about instilling values, honing skills, and fostering resilience, empowering students to make meaningful contributions to society.
