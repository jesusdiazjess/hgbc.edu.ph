ABOUT US

History

Established in 2005, HGBaquiran College stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of Tumaini, Isabela. With accreditation from both the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), our institution has consistently delivered exceptional learning experiences to students across various fields of study.

Our undergraduate programs span a diverse range, including teacher preparation, business administration, criminology, information technology, and hotel and restaurant management. Additionally, we offer a Senior High School curriculum tailored to meet the demands of the modern workforce, featuring a Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track with specialized strands in Higher Education (HE) and Information and Communications Technology (ICT).

Dedication, diligence, determination, devotion, and discipline are the guiding principles that underpin every facet of our institution. With these values at the forefront of our educational philosophy, we strive to inspire and equip our students with the tools they need to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

At HGBaquiran College, our ethos revolves around the cultivation of disciplined minds and the nurturing of globally competitive individuals. Our mission is rooted in the belief that education is not just about imparting knowledge but also about instilling values, honing skills, and fostering resilience to empower students to make meaningful contributions to society.
