About Us


Overview

HGBaquiran College, established in 2005, is a prominent educational institution located in Tumauini, Isabela, Philippines. Recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, the college is accredited by both the Department of Education (DepEd) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED). Over the years, it has built a reputation for providing high-quality education across a variety of disciplines, catering to the needs of students seeking both undergraduate and senior high school education.


HGBaquiran College offers a wide array of undergraduate programs, including degrees in teacher education, business administration, criminology, information technology, and hospitality management. In addition to its higher education offerings, the college provides a Senior High School program aligned with the K-12 curriculum. This program features a Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track, with specialized strands in Higher Education (HE) and Information and Communications Technology (ICT), designed to prepare students for the demands of the modern workforce.

Guided by the core values of dedication, diligence, determination, devotion, and discipline, HGBaquiran College emphasizes the holistic development of its students. These principles form the foundation of the institution's educational philosophy, which seeks to inspire learners and equip them with the skills necessary to succeed in a rapidly changing world. The college is dedicated to cultivating disciplined minds and nurturing globally competitive individuals who are prepared to face future challenges.

HGBaquiran College operates with the belief that education extends beyond the mere transmission of knowledge. Its mission is to instill values, hone practical skills, and foster resilience in students, empowering them to make meaningful contributions to society. By combining academic rigor with character formation, the college aims to produce graduates who are not only professionally competent but also socially responsible and ethically grounded.

Through its commitment to excellence and innovation, HGBaquiran College continues to play a vital role in shaping the educational landscape of Tumauini, Isabela, and beyond.
