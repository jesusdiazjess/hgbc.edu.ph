St. Paul University Philippines Hosts Regional ITE Convention 2025

March 9, 2025


St. Paul University Philippines (SPUP) is proud to announce the upcoming Regional ITE Convention 2025, set to take place from March 26 to 28, 2025. This highly anticipated event will bring together industry leaders, professionals, and students to explore the latest trends and innovations in information technology and engineering.

The convention will feature a dynamic lineup of activities, including keynote speeches, panel discussions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities. Attendees will gain insights from renowned speakers like Dr. Rocela Angelica B. Gorospe, Engr. Adam C. Abinales, and Dr. Mary Jane Calagui, who will cover topics ranging from artificial intelligence and cybersecurity to renewable energy and sustainable infrastructure. In addition to the thought-provoking sessions, the event will host exciting competitions, including ITE-CON-INNOVATE, Mobile Legends tournaments, Tower and Bridge Building challenges, and coding competitions. These activities aim to foster creativity, collaboration, and technical skills among participants.

Sister Merceditas O. Ang, SPC, University President of SPUP, expressed her enthusiasm for the event, stating, “This convention is a testament to our commitment to innovation and excellence. We look forward to welcoming participants from across the region to share knowledge and inspire one another.”

Whether you’re a seasoned professional or a student eager to learn, the Regional ITE Convention 2025 is an enriching experience. Don’t miss this opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and explore the future of technology and engineering

Registration is now open! Visit the official ITE Convention 2025 website to secure your spot and join the community of innovators. ITE Convention 2025
