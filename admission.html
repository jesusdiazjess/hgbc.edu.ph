Welcome

HGBaquiran College extends a warm welcome to senior high school graduates, college transferees, second coursers, and foreign applicants, inviting them to explore and join our vibrant campus. Submission of the following requirements to the Registrar's Office is mandatory for admission to any academic program:

Consistent with the objective of producing quality graduates, HGBaquiran College strictly screens all student applicants for admission. A student can only be admitted to HGBaquiran College if he or she possesses the following basic requirements:

1. HS Report Card and Forms 137 and 138-A
2. Certificate of good moral character
3. 3 pieces of 2x2 colored pictures
4. Birth Certificate (PSA-authenticated)
5. Police or NBI clearance may also be asked in special cases.
6. Certificate for a drug test.

• If you are a former student (students who have missed one semester or more of classes at HGBaquiran College) and would like to return and continue your degree program, DO NOT APPLY ONLINE. Instead, please download and fill-out a Re-entry Form. Please read page 2 of the form for former student admission requirements. Submit your completed form to the Registrar Office. 1. Transcript of records or true copies of grades. If official transcript of records in unavailable initially, Certification of Final grades of all subjects taken from the School Term must be submitted but the enrollee is considered as temporarily enrolled. The Official Transcript of Records must be submitted two(2) weeks from the start of the classes for the student to be considered permanently enrolled.
2. Honorable dismissal from the last school attended.
3. Certificate of good moral character from the last school attended.
