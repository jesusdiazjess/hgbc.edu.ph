Who are ACT?

HGBaquiran College’s Associate Computer Technology (ACT) program is crafted to provide a solid foundation in the dynamic realm of IT. Our curriculum spans essential topics like programming, network systems, cybersecurity, and IT support, combining theoretical insights with practical experience. Students benefit from modern facilities and hands-on learning, gaining skills that meet industry demands. The ACT program fosters critical thinking and problem-solving, encouraging students to tackle real-world tech challenges and explore innovative solutions. Emphasizing ethical technology use, the program prepares students to address ethical dilemmas and make a positive impact in the IT field. Whether your goal is to become a network administrator, IT technician, or software developer, our ACT program equips you for a successful career in technology.

Begin your journey with us and step into a world of technological possibilities.
