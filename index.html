Our Philosophy, Vision and Mission

HGBaquiran College, inspired by Tryon Edwards, focuses on disciplined education. It envisions well-rounded graduates for global competitiveness and active contributions. The mission is to provide quality education, shaping individuals with the skills and values for a changing world and nation-building.

PHILOSOPHY
HGBaquiran College adopts its philosophy from that of Thyron Edward, American Theologian, which states thus; “The great end of education is, to discipline rather than to furnish the mind; to train it to the use of its own powers, rather than fill it with accumulation of others”

VISION
A well rounded human being. Equipped with the values, attitudes and skills that will enable him/her to be locally and globally competitive, live a productive life, contribute to nation building, and meet creatively and resourcefully the social and moral challenges of the world.

MISSION
HGBaquiran College commits itself to offer quality education that would develop a well rounded human being who possesses desirable values, attitudes and skills thereby enabling him/her to be locally competitive, live a productive life, cope with the social and moral challenges of the modern world and be able to contribute to nation building.

Dedication, Deligence, Determination, Devotion and Discipline.
