Our Philosophy, Vision and Mission

HGBaquiran College, inspired by Tryon Edwards, focuses on disciplined education. It envisions well-rounded graduates for global competitiveness and active contributions.
The mission is to provide quality education, shaping individuals with the skills and values for a changing world and nation-building.


HGBaquiran College adopts its philosophy from that of Tryon Edwards, an American theologian, who stated: “The great end of education is to discipline rather than to furnish the mind; to train it to the use of its own powers rather than to fill it with the accumulation of others.”

A well-rounded human being. Equipped with the values, attitudes, and skills that will enable him/her to be locally and globally competitive, live a productive life, contribute to nation-building, and meet creatively and resourcefully the social and moral challenges of the world.

HGBaquiran College commits itself to offering quality education that would develop a well-rounded human being who possesses desirable values, attitudes, and skills, thereby enabling him/her to be locally competitive, live a productive life, cope with the social and moral challenges of the modern world, and be able to contribute to nation-building.



Dedication, Diligence, Determination, Devotion, and Discipline.

About Us

HGBaquiran College, Inc., is located in Tumauini, Isabela, Philippines 3325. It was established in 2005 and was the town of Tumauini's only college.
