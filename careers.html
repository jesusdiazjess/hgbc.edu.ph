Career Opportunities


At HGBaquiran College, the opportunities for career growth are many and wide-ranging. If you’re talented, proactive, and excited about having a fulfilling career in an organization with ambitious and attainable goals, we look forward to hearing from you.

We are in urgent need of competitive and career-driven individuals for the following positions:

Updated: July 2025


Job Description
Responsible for analyzing, designing, developing, implementing, and maintaining information systems. S/he works closely with stakeholders to understand their requirements and deliver to them the appropriate solution.

Qualifications
  • • Bachelor's degree of Science in Information Technology or Computer Science
  • • With hands-on experience on programming using .NET or JAVA technologies
  • • With at least one (1) year experience in software development
  • • Knowledge of Software Development Processes and Methodologies like Waterfall or Agile and skills using MSSQL Server, Oracle or MySQL, SSRS/Crystal Reports, .NET or JAVA Technologies
  • • Highly organized, detail-oriented, and capable of managing multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment
  • • Demonstrated ability to work independently, as well as part of a collaborative team
  • • Ability to work under pressure, meet deadlines, and handle sensitive and confidential information with discretion
  • • Willing to report onsite from Monday to Friday

Availability: Not Available / Occupied


Job Description
Responsible for the installation, maintenance, and repair of air conditioning systems, including diagnosing malfunctions, performing preventive maintenance, and providing customer education on proper equipment operation and maintenance.

Qualifications
  • • Preferably a Bachelor's or Vocational degree graduate (Diploma programs/courses under TESDA)
  • • Preferably with at least 2-3 years of experience as an Aircon Technician, preferably in the air conditioning and refrigeration industry
  • • Strong technical knowledge of AC systems, refrigeration principles, and electrical components
  • • Relevant certifications or licenses in air conditioning and refrigeration
  • • Amenable to work on a shifting schedule, 6 days a week, weekends/ holidays

Availability: Available


You may send your resume, transcript of records & 2x2 photo to:

Human Resources and Organizational Development
HGBaquiran College, Inc.
District 1 Centro, Tumauini, Isabela 3325

Or e-mail the requirements to: recruitment@hgbci.net
